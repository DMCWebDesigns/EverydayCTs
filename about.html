EverydayCTS began as a vision to create a space where Christians from all walks of life could come together to share their stories authentically and vulnerably. We recognized the importance of providing a platform for individuals to express their doubts, questions, and uncertainties, as well as their moments of faith, hope, and joy.

As we embarked on this journey, we encountered challenges, setbacks, and obstacles along the way. But through perseverance, determination, and unwavering faith, we remained committed to our mission of creating a community of authenticity, empathy, and mutual support.
